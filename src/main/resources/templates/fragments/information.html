These are the different ways you can use this app!

The different controls

Buttons!

    

At the bottom of the page you will find these buttons. These buttons will allow you vote either yes the scan is good or no the scan is bad

You can use your keyboard!

If you have a keyboard you can use the left and right arrows keys on the keyboard to rate the images. Left is for no this is not a good scan and right is for yes this is a good scan

You can swipe!

You can also swipe on the card shown to rate the images. Left is for a bad scan and right is a good scan
