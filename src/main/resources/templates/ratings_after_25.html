The Temporal Lobe

Great work! You just unlocked the temporal lobe. The temporal is involved in perceiving and remembering sounds. It also works in conjunction with the hippocampus to form conscious memories.

The case of Henry Molaison 1926-2008
From an early age, Henry suffered from severe epilepsy. In an effort to control his seizures, he had an operation that removed a large part of his temporal lobe and hippocampus on both sides. Whilst the surgery cured him of his epilepsy, tragically he was left unable to form new memories.
